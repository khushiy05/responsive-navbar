Burj Khalifa,Dubai

  • 📍 Location: Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE)
  • 📏 Height: 828 meters (2,717 feet)
  • 🏗️ Construction Period: 2004 - 2010
  • 🎉 Opening Date: January 4, 2010
  • 👷 Architect: Adrian Smith (Skidmore, Owings & Merrill)
  • 🏢 Floors: 163 (plus maintenance levels)

Mount-Fuji,Japan

  • 📍 Location: Honshu, Japan
  • 📏 Height: 3,776 meters (12,389 feet) – Japan’s tallest mountain
  • 🌋 Type: Stratovolcano (dormant but active)
  • 🗓️ Last Eruption: 1707-1708 (Hōei Eruption)
  • 🛤️ First Ascent: 663 AD (by a Buddhist monk)
  • ⛩️ Cultural Significance: Sacred site in Shinto and Buddhist traditions

Gyeongbokgung Palace,seoul,Korea

  • 📍 Location: 161 Sajik-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea
  • 🏗️ Construction Year: 1395 (Joseon Dynasty)
  • 👑 Founder: King Taejo
  • 📜 Historical Events: Destroyed during Japanese invasions (1592), restored in the 19th century, damaged during Japanese occupation (1910-1945), ongoing restoration since 1990.

Pharaohs of Ancient Egypt 🇪🇬

  • 📍 Civilization: Ancient Egypt
  • 👑 Title: Pharaoh (King of Upper and Lower Egypt)
  • 📜 Dynastic Period: c. 3100 BCE – 30 BCE
  • 🏗️ Notable Constructions: Pyramids, Temples, Sphinx, Valley of the Kings
  • 🪦 Burial Sites: Pyramids of Giza, Valley of the Kings, Saqqara

Petronas Towers, Malaysia

  • 📍 Location: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
  • 🏗️ Architectural Style: Postmodern with Islamic-inspired design
  • 📏 Height: 451.9 meters (1,483 feet)
  • 🏗️ Notable Constructions: Pyramids, Temples, Sphinx, Valley of the Kings
  • 🏢 Notable Features: Twin towers, Skybridge (41st & 42nd floors), 88 floors
